Wait.A Dessert Cafe?

When we set out to open in Virar, it was decided over a warm cup of chocolate that we would combine Parisian pâtisserie refinement with retro American dessert parlour indulgence. And as soon as we put 'Cocoa' and 'Dessert' together, the menu basically wrote itself. There is a strong connective line between fine French pastry and comforting diner desserts: pure joy. It was easy to see how happily an elegant Belgian chocolate soufflé would share menu space with warm fudge brownies and vanilla ice cream, or how natural it seemed to have a choice between a classic hot chocolate and an over-the-top loaded milkshake. It all comes together in a beautifully lit, cozy room in Virar West. Our main goal is simple: we just want you to have a nice time.
